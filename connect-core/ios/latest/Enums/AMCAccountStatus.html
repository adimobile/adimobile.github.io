public enum AMCAccountStatus

AMCAccountStatus contains all possible status for an account.

  • Exists: Email exists in SCV as Identification Email.
  • DoesNotExist: Email does not exists in SCV.
  • ExistsBlocked: Email exists and recipient is blocked.
